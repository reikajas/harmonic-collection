Act III: The Impetus.

Someone wise once said: It's about drive, it's about power,

we stay hungry, we devour.

Indeed, one must have a purpose to propel them through life.

What is your purpose?


a

 to provide unconditional support to your loved one.

b

 to explore all that the world has to offer.

c

 to eat and have fun as much as the heart desires.

d

 to try every type of smoked salmon there is.

e

 to mess around with people for fun.

f

 to simply live life to the fullest.
